# Mastering Prompt Engineering with Google's Framework

[Google's 6 Hour Prompt Engineering Course in 10 Minutes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=o3qfL2fcSx4)

![image1](../../resources/d370465032f04535b0228abfcb7f5dce.png)
Understanding the Core Principles of Prompting
- Google's prompt engineering course is structured around five core principles:**task, context, references, evaluate, and iterate**.
- The fundamental element is the**task**, which defines the exact output you want the AI to produce, not just the general topic .
- A basic task like "help me with email" can be significantly improved by specifying the desired output, such as "reformatting the sentence to write an email to my gym staff about a schedule change" .
Enhancing Tasks with Persona and Format
- Adding a**persona**primes the AI to access a specific vocabulary and logic, leading to more tailored responses .
- For example, instructing the AI to act as a physical therapist when asking for a workout plan ensures safety tips and anatomical focus are included .
- Defining the**format**(e.g., bulleted list, markdown table, JSON snippet) prevents generic text outputs and forces the AI to organize its thinking, resulting in usable deliverables .
- Combining task, persona, and format transforms basic requests into structured, specific results .
Leveraging Context for Targeted Outputs
- **Context**provides the background data that steers the AI's response; the more information provided, the less the AI needs to guess .
- Simply asking for "landing page copy" yields generic text, but providing context about the product, target audience, and desired tone results in a much more targeted output .
- Many users skip providing context, assuming the AI will infer it, which often leads to suboptimal results .
Using References to Guide AI Style and Structure
- **References**, or examples, demonstrate the desired vibe or structure when words alone are insufficient .
- When aiming for a specific brand voice in a product description, pasting in examples of your best descriptions and instructing the AI to match the style is more effective than just explaining the tone .
- Similarly, feeding the AI top-performing social media posts allows it to analyze successful patterns and generate new content that follows that exact structure .
- References prevent the AI from guessing your style and ensure it matches what you already know works .
Evaluating and Iterating for Improved Results
- **Evaluate**involves actively checking if the AI's output meets the task requirements, tone, and accuracy of data .
- Many users fail to evaluate thoroughly, settling for "good enough" .
- **Iterate**is the process of refining prompts through a loop of asking, checking, adjusting, and asking again .
Strategies for Fixing Broken Prompts
- **Revisit the framework**: Check if context or persona was missed in the initial prompt .
- **Break into simpler sentences**: Overwhelming the AI with a massive paragraph of instructions can lead to poor processing; breaking down complex requests into clearer, structured sentences improves output .
- **Use analogous tasks**: If a direct approach fails, try a different angle or frame to change the AI's mental model .
- **Add constraints**: Imposing specific requirements, such as length or focus, can force creativity and lead to more specific ideas rather than broad ones .
Multimodal Prompting: Beyond Text
- Multimodal prompting allows AI models to process images, audio, and video natively, going beyond text-based interactions .
- For website redesign feedback, uploading a screenshot and asking the AI to identify areas of user attention drop-off and suggest improvements is more efficient than describing the layout in words .
- Musicians can upload audio files and ask the AI to describe the mood and suggest arrangement directions .
- The core prompting framework still applies, but with high-fidelity visual or audio references replacing text descriptions .
Addressing Hallucinations and Bias in AI Models
- AI models can suffer from**hallucinations**, confidently inventing false information .
- An example is an AI incorrectly stating the number of 'e's in a word, as it predicts patterns rather than performing literal counts .
- **Bias**is absorbed from the open internet, embedding human prejudices like gender bias, racial stereotypes, and cultural assumptions into the training data .
- Google's solution is the**human in the loop**concept, where users act as a safety net to verify claims and question assumptions, rather than trusting the AI blindly .
Practical Applications of Prompt Engineering
- For freelance consultants, creating a master prompt for common onboarding questions (e.g., project timeline, required information, communication channels) can save significant time compared to manual responses .
- Google's course offers scenarios for various tasks, from cold outreach to meeting summaries .
Advanced Prompting Techniques
- **Prompt chaining**involves using the output of one prompt as the input for the next, building complexity layer by layer for complex projects .
- An example is launching a podcast: first generate names, then taglines for the top names, and finally a full launch plan using the selected name and tagline .
- **Chain of thought prompting**requires the AI to explain its reasoning step-by-step, helping to identify flawed logic .
- When deciding on app pricing models, asking the AI to walk through its reasoning for each option, considering factors like user psychology and revenue sustainability, provides deeper insight .
- **Tree of thought prompting**explores multiple reasoning paths simultaneously, ideal for complex problems like creative projects or strategic decisions .
- For designing a mobile app onboarding flow, generating different approaches (e.g., speed, education, personalization) and evaluating their user experience and potential dropoff points allows for exploration of multiple branches .
AI Agents: Specialized Tools for High-Value Tasks
- **AI agents**are specialized personas designed for specific, high-value tasks .
- **Simulation agents**act as practice partners for running live scenarios, such as mock interviews or sales calls, providing feedback on performance .
- **Expert feedback agents**function as personal consultants, critiquing work based on expert principles and suggesting improvements, such as refining cold email templates for better conversion rates .
- Google provides a blueprint for building these agents by defining a persona, injecting context, specifying the interaction, and setting a stop phrase .
Metaprompting: The Ultimate Prompting Cheat Code
- **Metaprompting**involves using the AI to improve your own prompts, essentially prompting about prompting .
- This technique ensures you get the best results, even when unsure how to phrase a request .
Conclusion: The Prompt Engineering Workflow
- The core workflow involves defining the**task**, setting the**context**, providing**references**, and then**evaluating**and**iterating**.
- This structured approach differentiates users who find AI ineffective from those who leverage it to save significant time .
- Moving beyond single-sentence prompts to building prompts layer by layer is key to utilizing AI effectively .
