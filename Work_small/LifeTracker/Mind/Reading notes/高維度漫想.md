<th><p><strong>INPUT</strong></p>
<p><em><span style='color:#595959'>輸入: 知覺</span></em></p>
<ul>
<li><p>意義形成: 知覺-&gt;解釋-&gt;賦予意義</p></li>
<li><p>Every medium hides as much as it reveals — the blindspot <em>is</em> the format</p></li>
<li><p>Switching formats is thinking, not translating</p></li>
<li><p>Explicit formats (language, code, math) sacrifice simultaneity for precision</p></li>
<li><p>Tacit knowledge (body, sensation, metaphor) shapes thought before words arrive</p></li>
<li><p>When stuck, change the representation — you're trapped in a blindspot</p></li>
<li><p>The medium doesn't carry thought; it sculpts what becomes thinkable</p></li>
</ul></th>
