Below is a **complete, self-contained life guidance system** designed to be *adopted immediately*—mentally and behaviorally—by an INFP who values meaning, struggles with overthinking, and seeks stable action across body, mind, social life, time, and money.
It is scientifically grounded, philosophically coherent, and explicitly engineered to reduce internal friction while preserving depth.

**I. The Core System: Meaning-through-Flow, Stability-through-Action**
**The Central Axiom (Memorize This)**
**Meaning is not discovered by thinking; it is generated by sustained, value-aligned action under manageable constraint.**
This system rejects the INFP trap of *waiting for inner clarity*. Clarity is treated as a **byproduct**, not a prerequisite.

**II. The Five-Domain Wealth Model (Your Operating Frame)**
You will evaluate life through **five forms of wealth**, each necessary and mutually reinforcing:
1.  **Physical Wealth** – energy, health, nervous system stability
2.  **Psychological Wealth** – emotional regulation, self-trust, internal coherence
3.  **Social Wealth** – reliable relationships, low-friction cooperation
4.  **Temporal Wealth** – usable, undrained time and attention
5.  **Financial Wealth** – optionality, safety, future leverage
**Rule:**
No decision is judged by “how it feels now,” but by whether it *increases at least one form of wealth without catastrophically depleting the others*.

**III. A Redefinition of Core Human Phenomena (Inside This System)**
**1. Meaning**
Meaning is **experienced coherence over time** between:
- what you value,
- what you repeatedly do,
- and what accumulates as a result.
It is **not** a feeling. It is a *pattern you can recognize in hindsight*.
**2. Emotion**
Emotions are **state signals**, not truth signals.
They inform you about energy, safety, and alignment—but they do not command action.
**3. Motivation**
Motivation is **dopamine-mediated expectancy**, not moral strength.
You do not “become motivated”; you **design environments where motivation emerges automatically**.
**4. Love**
Love is **voluntary commitment to another’s flourishing over time**, constrained by self-preservation.
It is not emotional intensity; it is *repeated choice under imperfection*.
**5. Purpose**
Purpose is not a destination.
Purpose is the **direction of effort you are willing to continue even when feedback is delayed or ambiguous**.

**IV. The Anti-Overthinking Engine (Why This Works for INFPs)**
**The Overthinking Loop (What You Are Escaping)**
- Excessive simulation → uncertainty → emotional ambiguity → action paralysis
- Dopamine suppressed due to low reward predictability
- Self-trust erodes because intentions do not convert into evidence
**The Replacement Loop (What You Will Run Instead)**
1.  **Small action**
2.  **Immediate completion**
3.  **Visible trace**
4.  **Reward prediction error resolved**
5.  **Self-trust incremented**
Over time, *action becomes emotionally safe again*.

**V. The System Rules (Non-Negotiable)**
**Rule 1: Action Precedes Meaning**
You are forbidden from asking “Is this meaningful?” **before** doing something small and bounded.
**Rule 2: Psychological Stability Is a First-Class Asset**
If an action costs you days of rumination, it is too expensive—even if it is “meaningful.”
**Rule 3: No Zero Days (But Micro Days Are Allowed)**
Any day with at least **one intentional action** counts.
**Rule 4: Financial Safety Is Ethical**
Money is not greed; it is **reduced cognitive load and future freedom**.

**VI. Daily Operating Protocol (Baseline Version)**
**Morning (5–10 minutes)**
- Identify **one action** today that:
  - is concrete,
  - can be completed in \<45 minutes,
  - increases *any* one wealth domain.
Write it down. That is the day’s success condition.
**During the Day**
- When hesitation arises, say internally:  
  **“Clarity is earned post-action.”**
- Start the action *badly*.
**Evening (5 minutes)**
- Log:
  - Action completed
  - Which wealth domain increased
  - One sentence: “Today mattered because \_\_\_.”
No emotional analysis. Only record.

**VII. Measurement (How You Know It’s Working)**
**Psychological Wealth Metrics (Weekly)**
- Days with completed intentional action (target: ≥5/7)
- Average rumination duration (subjective, trending downward)
- Self-trust score (1–10): “I do what I say I’ll do.”
**Financial Wealth Metrics (Monthly)**
- Emergency buffer size
- Income streams count
- Hours traded per dollar earned (trend, not perfection)
