Search: i want to find pertuzumab sequence used clinically, where to search for research
Pertuzumab (Perjeta) is a humanized monoclonal antibody targeting HER2 domain II, with sequences available through pharmaceutical patent applications and biological databases. Key sources include the RCSB Protein Data Bank (PDB) 9L1S, FDA/EMA drug approval documents, and PubMed for structure-function studies.
Where to Search for Pertuzumab Sequences & Research
RCSB Protein Data Bank (PDB): Use PDB IDs (e.g., 9L1S for a variant structure or 4H26) to find the precise amino acid sequences of the heavy and light chains.
PubMed Central (PMC): Search for "Pertuzumab" and "structure" or "sequence" to find peer-reviewed journal articles detailing its binding mechanism and sequence.
World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) Patentscope: Search original patent applications by Genentech/Roche for the definitive sequence disclosures.
FDA/EMA Drug Approvals (Roche/Genentech): Official documentation provides detailed information on the drug’s composition.
ClinicalTrials.gov: Identifies clinical trials (e.g., NCT00545688, NCT00567190) that used pertuzumab, which reference the pharmaceutical agent, Pertuzumab (Perjeta).

Search: how to search mab drug that target a specific protein
1\. 查詢「已核准」或「臨床開發中」的藥物
如果您想找的是已經進入人體試驗或上市的治療用抗體：
YAbS (The Antibody Society)：這是最權威的資源，收錄了超過 2,900 種進入臨床研究的抗體，包含分子結構、目標抗原及開發進度。
DrugBank Online：在搜尋框輸入蛋白質名稱（如 PD-1 或 TNF-alpha），即可在 "Targets" 欄位查看相關的單株抗體清單。
Wikipedia - List of therapeutic monoclonal antibodies：提供詳盡的表格，按字母順序排列抗體名稱、目標抗原與核准狀況。
2\. 查詢「研究級」或「診斷用」抗體
如果您需要的是實驗室研究用的抗體工具：
CiteAb：全球最大的抗體搜尋引擎，可以根據引用文獻量來排序針對特定蛋白質最有效的抗體。
BenchSci：利用 AI 從學術期刊中提取抗體使用數據，幫助研究人員確認該抗體在特定實驗（如 Western Blot 或 IHC）中的表現。
3\. 生物資訊與序列比對工具
如果您持有蛋白質序列，想預測或確認抗體結合位點：
NCBI BLAST：可用於鑑定蛋白質變體，並找出與其他蛋白質共有的序列，藉此預測抗體可能的交叉反應性。
IMGT/mAb-DB：專注於抗體序列、結構及免疫遺傳學數據的專業數據庫。
