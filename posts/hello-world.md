## Getting started

Posts are plain **Markdown** files stored in your GitHub repository. To publish a post:

1. Add the file to your repo (anywhere — root or a subfolder)
2. Open the ⚙ admin panel on your blog
3. Toggle the post **on** and hit **Save**

The blog will update within about a minute.

## Frontmatter

You can add a YAML header at the top of any post to set the title and date:

```markdown
---
title: My Post Title
date: 2026-04-29
---

Post content goes here…
```

If there's no frontmatter, the title is derived from the filename and no date is shown.
