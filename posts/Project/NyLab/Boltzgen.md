Boltzgen
Sunday, February 8, 2026
3:43 PM

- **BoltzGen**is a new generative model for designing proteins and peptides of any modality to bind a wide range of biomolecular targets.
- It**unifies design and structure prediction**, resulting in a single model that also achieves state-of-the-art folding performance. BoltzGen's generation process can be controlled with a flexible design specification language.
- BoltzGen was developed at MIT and experimentally validated in a large-scale distributed effort involving multiple academic and industry labs.
- We explicitly focus our experimental validation on targets that are highly dissimilar to any proteins for which bound structures exist.
- BoltzGen, like Boltz-1 and Boltz-2, is open source under MIT license and freely available for**unrestricted academic and commercial use**.
