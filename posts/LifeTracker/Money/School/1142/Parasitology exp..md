Strongyloides stercoralis and hookworm larvae (rhabditiform) are differentiated under a microscope by their buccal cavity length, genital primordium, and tail shape. S. stercoralis has a short buccal cavity, prominent genital primordium, and a notched tail. Hookworms have a long buccal cavity, small genital primordium, and a pointed tail.
Ascaris lumbricoides eggs are differentiated microscopically by shape, shell thickness, and internal content. Fertilized eggs are round/oval, have a thick shell with a prominent bumpy (mammillated) outer layer, and contain a large, single-cell embryo. Unfertilized eggs are longer/narrower, with a thinner shell, less distinct mammillation, and disorganized, refractile granules inside.

在閱讀清單前，需先釐清教材對宿主的定義：
- **終宿主 (Definitive Host, FH)：** 寄生蟲成蟲或行有性生殖階段的宿主。
- **中間宿主 (Intermediate Host, IH)：** 幼蟲或行無性生殖階段的宿主。
- **保蟲宿主 (Reservoir Host, RH)：** 寄生蟲可同時寄生於人與動物，且動物可將病原傳給人。
- **轉續/保幼宿主 (Transport/Paratenic Host, PH)：** 幼蟲進入非正常宿主後不發育，但保持感染力，進入正常宿主後才發育為成蟲。

**一、 線蟲 (Nematodes)**

**三、 其他重要分類特徵 (補充來源資訊)**
- **自我感染 (Auto-infection) 名單：** 蟯蟲、糞小桿線蟲、菲律賓毛線蟲、有鉤絛蟲、短小包膜絛蟲、隱孢子蟲。
- **無需中間宿主：** 腸道常見線蟲（蛔、鞭、蟯、鉤、糞小桿）均標註為「無中間宿主」，屬於土源性 (Soil-transmitted)。
- **幼蟲穿透皮膚感染：** 鉤蟲、糞小桿線蟲、棘口線蟲。

帶蟲免疫（Premunition），又稱「感染免疫」，是一種寄生蟲病特有的非消除性免疫狀態。宿主體內殘留低密度寄生蟲，雖然未能完全清除感染，但能有效對抗同種寄生蟲的再次感染，維持無症狀或輕症狀態。此狀態隨寄生蟲存在而持續，蟲體消失則免疫力通常會下降。
Based on the provided sources, the treatment for various parasitic diseases can be summarized as follows:
**1. Protozoan Diseases**
- **Malaria:**
  - **To alleviate symptoms** (blood stage): Chloroquine, quinine, pyrimethamine-sulfadoxine (PS), and artemisinin.
  - **To prevent relapse** (*P. vivax/ovale*): **Primaquine** is used to target hypnozoites.
  - **To prevent spread** (gametocytes): Primaquine for *P. falciparum*; Chloroquine for others.
  - **Artemisinin Combination Therapy (ACT):** Recommended to prevent drug resistance; examples include Winthrop (artesunate/amodiaquine) and Coartem (artemether/lumefantrine).
- **Toxoplasmosis:**
  - **Immunocompetent/Symptomatic:** Pyrimethamine, sulfadiazine, and folinic acid.
  - **Pregnant Women:** Spiramycin (1st trimester); Pyrimethamine/sulfadiazine/folinic acid (2nd/3rd trimesters).
  - **Ocular/Immunocompromised:** Trimethoprim/sulfamethoxazole.
- **African Sleeping Sickness:**
  - **甘比亞錐蟲 (West African):** Early stage uses **Pentamidine**; late stage uses **NECT** (Eflornithine + Nifurtimox).
  - **羅德西亞錐蟲 (East African):** Early stage uses **Suramin**; late stage uses **Melarsoprol**.
- **Amoebiasis (*E. histolytica*):**
  - **Acute/Extra-intestinal:** Metronidazole (Flagyl) followed by **luminal agents** like iodoquinol, paromomycin, or diloxanide furoate.
  - **Severe cases:** Dehydroemetine may be used (but is contraindicated in pregnancy).
- **Giardiasis:** Metronidazole, Albendazole, Furazolidone, Nitazoxanide, or Tinidazole. Patients should also maintain fluid replacement to prevent dehydration.
- **Cryptosporidiosis:** There is **no fully effective therapy**, but **Nitazoxanide** is the only approved antiparasitic drug. For AIDS patients, antiretroviral therapy (ART) is essential to strengthen the immune system.
- **Cyclosporiasis & Isosporiasis:** Both are primarily treated with **trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole** (TMP-SMZ).
- **Sarcocystosis:** Usually requires no treatment, but severe muscular cases may use **Albendazole** or cotrimoxazole, along with corticosteroids to reduce inflammation.
- **Microsporidiosis: Albendazole** is used for most species; ocular infections are coupled with **topical Fumagillin**. Metronidazole or Thalidomide may be used as alternatives.
- **Trichomoniasis: Metronidazole** or **Tinidazole** (often as a single dose).
**2. Helminthic (Worm) Diseases**
- **Intestinal Nematodes (Ascaris, Hookworm, Whipworm):**
  - **Mebendazole** or **Albendazole** are the primary drugs of choice.
  - **Pyrantel pamoate** is an alternative given as a single dose.
  - **Hookworm-specific:** Severe anemia must be treated with **oral iron replacement** (e.g., ferrous sulphate).
- **Enterobiasis (Pinworm):** Mebendazole or pyrantel pamoate.
- **Strongyloidiasis: Ivermectin** is the first-line treatment; Albendazole is the second-line.
- **Toxocariasis:** Albendazole.
- **Cutaneous Larva Migrans (CLM):** Treated with oral **Ivermectin**, **Albendazole**, or **Thiabendazole**; topical 15% Thiabendazole ointment can also be applied.
- **Anisakiasis:** Acute cases require **endoscopic removal** of the larvae; chronic cases may require surgical excision.
**3. Important Treatment Precautions**
- **Pregnancy:** Mebendazole and Albendazole are contraindicated in pregnancy. Metronidazole should be avoided during the first trimester.
- **Alcohol:** Patients must **avoid alcohol** while taking Metronidazole or Tinidazole to prevent a **disulfiram-like reaction** (symptoms include nausea, headache, and rapid heartbeat).
- **Anemia Management:** In heavy hookworm infections, anemia should be stabilized with iron before full antiparasitic treatment if hemoglobin levels are extremely low.

根據提供的教材來源，多種寄生蟲會透過侵入或破壞宿主的粘膜（mucosa）來致病。以下為具有**粘膜侵入或破壞特性**的寄生蟲整理：
**1. 腸道原蟲 (Intestinal Protozoa)**
- **痢疾阿米巴 (*Entamoeba histolytica*)：**
  - 滋養體利用半胱氨酸蛋白酶（cysteine proteinases/histolysins）降解細胞基質並**侵入組織**。
  - 它們會破壞粘膜並進入**粘膜下層（submucosa）**，在那裡橫向擴散，形成特徵性的**燒瓶狀潰瘍（flask-shaped ulcers）**。
- **大腸纖毛蟲 (*Balantidium coli*)：**
  - 它們通常居住在大腸腔內，但可以**穿透粘膜**並分泌物質破壞腸道組織，進而產生潰瘍。
- **藍氏賈第鞭毛蟲 (*Giardia lamblia*)：**
  - 滋養體會**附著於小腸上部的粘膜**上。
  - 雖然其機制主要被認為是機械性刺激或阻礙吸收，但會導致上皮損傷（epithelial damage），包括絨毛變鈍和隱窩細胞肥大。
**2. 組織與腸道線蟲 (Nematodes)**
- **糞小桿線蟲 (*Strongyloides stercoralis*)：**
  - 具有自我感染（autoinfection）的能力，幼蟲可以**重新侵入腸粘膜**或肛門周圍皮膚，進入循環系統。
- **菲律賓毛線蟲 (*Capillaria philippinensis*)：**
  - 其幼蟲也會**重新侵入小腸粘膜**，形成自我感染循環。
  - 重度感染會導致嚴重的蛋白質流失性腸病。
- **海獸胃線蟲 (*Anisakis spp.*)：**
  - 幼蟲會鑽入**胃壁或腸道的粘膜下層**，引起發炎、出血、潰瘍或肉芽腫。
- **鞭蟲 (*Trichuris trichiura*)：**
  - 成蟲的前端（較細的部分）會**埋入大腸（盲腸）的粘膜**中固定。
- **鉤蟲 (*Hookworms*)：**
  - 成蟲附著於**小腸粘膜**上，並利用吸盤吸血，造成出血與潰瘍。
**3. 生殖泌尿道原蟲 (Urogenital Protozoa)**
- **陰道滴蟲 (*Trichomonas vaginalis*)：**
  - 雖然屬於胞外寄生，但它會**附著於上皮組織**並造成**上皮組織的侵蝕（erosion）**，導致陰道與子宮頸發炎及出現草莓狀外觀（strawberry cervix）。
**4. 其他特殊部位**
- **棘阿米巴 (*Acanthamoeba*)：**
  - 可沿著\*\*嗅覺神經上皮途徑（olfactory neuroepithelium route）\*\*侵入中樞神經系統。
- **等孢子蟲 (*Isospora belli*) 與 隱孢子蟲 (*Cryptosporidium parvum*)：**
  - 這兩者的孢子小體（sporozoites）會進入並**寄生於小腸上皮細胞**內進行發育與繁殖。
**總結：** 在臨床上，最常被強調具有強力「粘膜侵入性」並導致潰瘍的是**痢疾阿米巴**與**大腸纖毛蟲**；而具有「反覆侵入粘膜」完成自我感染循環的則是**糞小桿線蟲**與**菲律賓毛線蟲**。
